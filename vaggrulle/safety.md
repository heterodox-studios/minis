# VÄGGRULLE — Safety information

- Adult installation. Not a toy.
- Fix securely to a suitable wall. Do not overload the shelves.
- Keep magnets out of reach of small children. Swallowing two or more magnets can cause serious internal injury — seek immediate medical help.
- Keep magnets away from pacemakers, other implanted medical devices, and magnetic storage media.
- Paper is flammable — keep flames and other heat sources away from the unit.

Report faults or incidents to Heterodox AB: info@heterodox.se

Complies with Regulation (EU) 2023/988 (GPSR). See the [Declaration of Conformity](./declaration-of-conformity.md).
