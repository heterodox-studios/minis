# VÄGGRULLE — Safety information

Please read this before installing or using your VÄGGRULLE.

## At a glance

- For adult use. Adult installation required. Not a toy.
- Mount securely to a suitable wall using appropriate fixings.
- Keep magnets out of reach of small children.
- Keep magnets away from pacemakers and other implanted medical devices, and from magnetic storage media.
- Paper is flammable — keep flames and other heat sources away from the unit.

## Installation

The unit must be securely fixed to a wall capable of supporting its weight (~2 kg empty, more when loaded with paper and items on the shelves).

The supplied screws are intended for wooden walls, thin sheet-metal walls, or plasterboard backed by plywood or OSB. For other wall types — masonry, concrete, unbacked plasterboard — please source appropriate wall plugs or fixings.

Installation involves drilling and screw-driving. Take normal precautions: check for hidden cables and pipes before drilling, wear eye protection, and keep children and pets clear of the work area.

See the separate [assembly instructions](./assembly-instructions.md) for the step-by-step procedure.

## During use

**Do not overload the shelves.** The shelves are sized for everyday writing accessories — pens, a phone, a coffee cup, post-it notes, permanent markers. They are not designed to carry heavy or bulky items.

**Keep flames and other sources of ignition away from the unit.** This includes lit candles, incense, tea-lights, hobs and electric heaters. Paper is flammable and the unit holds a substantial roll of it.

**Mind the small upper shelf.** It is intended for items you want kept out of children's reach, such as permanent markers. Mount the unit at a height that places this shelf above the reach of any children in the home.

## Magnets

Two small powder-coated magnets are supplied. They are placed on the writing surface to hold the paper flat against the wall, and removed when you want to advance the paper.

**Keep magnets out of reach of small children.** If two or more magnets are swallowed they can attract through the wall of the bowel, causing serious internal injury. Seek immediate medical attention if a magnet is swallowed.

**Keep magnets away from pacemakers, implanted defibrillators (ICDs) and other implanted medical devices.** Magnetic fields can interfere with these devices at close range.

**Keep magnets away from magnetic storage media** — credit cards, hotel key cards, hard disk drives, magnetic tapes — to avoid data loss.

When the unit is not in use, the magnets stay attracted to the steel parts behind the paper. If you remove them for any reason, store them somewhere children cannot reach.

## Cleaning

Wipe clean with a soft, damp cloth. Do not use abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals. Do not put any part in a dishwasher. Do not expose to high heat.
