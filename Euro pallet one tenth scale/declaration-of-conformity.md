### Product description:

A 1/10 scale model of a Euro pallet 3D printed from PLA with captive magnets.

Product is available in several colours. May be sold singly or in packs containing several.

Products have SKUs in the form “pallet-pla-CC-XX” where CC is colour code and XX is number of pallets in a pack. For example `pallet-pla-wh-01` is a single white pallet.
